## 🛡️ Privacy Policy

### 1. Ads and Data Collection

Copilot Chat Extractor uses advertising services provided by PlayaYield.  
These ads do not collect personal data from your device.

For more details, you can read PlayaYield’s official privacy policy:  
https://www.playayield.com/privacy

### 2. Copilot Data Handling

This extension interacts only with your local Copilot session to export your conversations.

- All Copilot content stays on your device.
- The extension does not send, store, upload, or share any Copilot data externally.
- No conversation content is transmitted to third‑party services.

### 3. Local Processing

- All operations (exporting MD/DOCX, UI integration, themes, menus) are performed locally inside your browser.
- The extension does not use remote servers, cloud storage, or external APIs to process your conversations.

### 4. Permissions

Copilot Chat Extractor™ only requests the permissions required to:

- Display its menu inside Copilot.
- Export conversations.
- Open Copilot in popup, tab, or side panel.
- Show ads inside Copilot.

No additional permissions are used.

### 5. Summary

Copilot Chat Extractor™ is designed with privacy first:

- No personal data collection.
- No external storage.
- No conversation sharing.
- No tracking.
- No analytics.
- Local processing only.
